1. Navigate to maps.google.com
2. If a consent or cookie banner appears, dismiss it by clicking "Accept all" or the equivalent.
3. Click the "Directions" button (it shows a diamond-shaped arrow icon) next to the main search box in the top-left panel.
4. In the starting-location field (top field of the directions panel), type "KAUST" and then click the matching "King Abdullah University of Science and Technology" suggestion from the dropdown.
5. In the destination field (bottom field of the directions panel), type "King Abdulaziz International Airport Jeddah" and then click the matching suggestion from the dropdown.
6. Click the car icon in the top of the directions panel to select driving mode.
7. Wait for the route to be drawn on the map and the estimated travel time to appear.
8. Verify that a driving route is shown on the map and a time estimate (for example "1 h 15 min") is visible in the results panel.
