1. Navigate to amazon.com
2. If a location or delivery-preference banner appears, dismiss it by clicking the close button or "Continue".
3. Click the main search input field at the top of the page.
4. Type "wireless mouse".
5. Press the Enter key to submit the search.
6. Wait for the search results grid to load.
7. Click on the title of the first search result that is NOT labeled "Sponsored".
8. Wait for the product detail page to fully load.
9. Click the yellow "Add to Cart" button on the right side of the product page.
10. Wait for the "Added to Cart" confirmation page or side panel to appear.
11. Verify that the cart icon in the top-right shows an item count of at least 1. Do NOT proceed to checkout.
