1. Navigate to youtube.com
2. If a "Before you continue" consent dialog appears, click "Accept all".
3. Click the search input field at the top of the YouTube header.
4. Type "lex fridman podcast".
5. Press the Enter key to submit the search.
6. Wait for the search results page to load.
7. Click the thumbnail of the first video result (skip any entries labeled as channels, shorts, or ads).
8. Verify that the video player opens and the video begins playing. If it does not auto-play, click the play button in the center of the video player.
